    Project Manager

    Project Manager

    W.S. Cumby Construction is seeking a self-starting, deadline-driven and detail-oriented individual who will be accountable for the overall project team leadership including management of an assigned project team, creating and maintaining strong relations with the client and design team and ensuring the project meets the established goals for safety, quality, schedule and profit. The ideal candidate must be able to demonstrate verifiable successful results in managing multiple high quality projects and possess a minimum of 5 years of relevant commercial construction experience. A Bachelor’s degree in construction management, architecture, engineering or equivalent is desired with proficiency in Procore, P6 Scheduling and Office.

    *We offer a competitive benefits package including insurance (health, dental, vision, life, disability)as well as paid holidays, paid vacation, paid sick time, 401K, and tuition reimbursement.

    To apply, please submit salary requirements, cover letter and resume to: hr@cumby.com.
    No phone calls please.

    W.S. Cumby is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

    Superintendent

    Assistant Project Manager

    Assistant Project Manager

    W.S. Cumby is seeking a self-starting, deadline-driven and detail-oriented individual who will work in collaboration with the Project Manager, Superintendent and other operations team members on assigned projects. The candidate should embody the company’s core values of Integrity, Partnership and Commitment.

    Function:

    This position requires the ability to expedite project schedules, material procurement, submittals, and shop drawings. The Assistant Project Manager must be a flexible and adaptable team-player, as well as having strong technical, computer, communication and client management skills.

    Responsibilities:

    Expedite, review for compliance and track status of submittals and shop drawings
    Initiate, prepare, review, track and distribute RFIs
    Update construction schedule based on procurement status, material fabrication and delivery, project team input and Trade Contractor progress
    Change management systems, including soliciting, receiving, reviewing, and preparing quotations and change orders
    Maintain construction drawing documents and log

    Qualifications:

    · Associate or Bachelor’s degree in engineering or architecture

    · Minimum 3 years prior commercial construction experience

    · Strong organizational, technical and time management skills

    Technical Skills:

    · CAD, Procore, Oracle P6, Office, Bluebeam, Revit and Navisworks a plus

    *We offer a competitive benefits package including insurance (health, dental, vision, life, disability)as well as paid holidays, paid vacation, paid sick time, 401K, and tuition reimbursement.

    To apply, please submit salary requirements, cover letter and resume to: hr@cumby.com.
    No phone calls please.

    W.S. Cumby is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
