    Profile

    Founded in 1981 by the late William S. Cumby, Sr. and William S. Cumby, Jr., W.S. Cumby provides a full spectrum of professional construction services including construction management, design/build and general contracting. Our client base has grown to encompass a diverse range of building sectors and we are proud to have a long list of repeat clients from the educational, religious, healthcare, commercial, retail, multi-family residential and hospitality fields. We are based in Springfield, PA and proudly serve the Greater Philadelphia and Tri-State areas.

    As a family owned business with strong family values, we are highly regarded for our integrity. Thanks to our team of uncommonly dedicated construction professionals, we’ve become renowned for our partnership-based approach to the planning, pre-construction and construction management of renovation, restoration, and construction projects.

    Delaware Valley Green Builders

    We have been leaders in the Green Building movement for more than a decade and, as a result, have built high-performance buildings for dozens of clients, helping them achieve their goals in a smart, affordable way. We are one of the largest Merit Shop firms in the Philadelphia area and have gained a particularly strong reputation for the extraordinary value of our pre-construction services. In addition, the importance we place on safety has resulted in us being invited back to work in occupied campuses and facilities many times by many repeat clients.

    Service, Quality & Value

    It is our mission to provide the best service in the construction industry. To that end, we believe that the quality of the process is equally important as the quality of the product. The result of this unfailing commitment to service is that we have successfully completed hundreds of projects approaching a billion dollars in value for hundreds of satisfied clients. Our goal for the future is to continually strive for excellence, to bring our uncompromising values and industry leading expertise to more and more delighted clients.

    Safety Program

    Our highest priority is to provide a healthy, safe, clean and enjoyable working environment for all of our staff, subcontractors, vendors and visitors. We take this responsibility seriously by hiring the best construction professionals, setting the highest standards, and embracing a culture of continual improvement.

    We manage a comprehensive safety program, enforced with stringent controls, that empowers all employees to become active stewards of safety. The foundation of our safety culture rests on the following principles:

    • Mandatory OSHA training and certification for all field staff
    • Top-to-bottom management commitment
    • Safety-focused pre-construction and construction meetings
    • Safety expectations in all subcontract agreements
    • High hazard pre-task planning
    • Regular third party professional job-site safety inspections
    • Consistent PPE requirements

    The results of our commitment to safety speak for themselves: We have maintained a  Workmen’s Compensation Experience Modification Factor  (EMR) that is far below the industry standard. We are founding members of General Contractors Insurance LTD, a “Best-in-Class” Captive Insurance Company composed of only the safest construction firms in the nation.
